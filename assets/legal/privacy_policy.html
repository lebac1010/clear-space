Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Clear Space accesses, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app.

Overview

Clear Space is a storage management and cleanup app. Its core functionality is to analyze files stored on your device, present cleanup suggestions, and perform cleanup actions that you explicitly request.

The app is designed so that storage scanning, duplicate detection, similar photo detection, junk review, download review, screenshot review, media browsing, and most related processing occur on your device.

Information the app may access on your device

  • Photos and image metadata, including filenames, sizes, timestamps, and content URIs, so the app can show photo storage usage, duplicate files, similar photos, screenshots, and large image files.
  • Videos and video metadata, including filenames, sizes, timestamps, and content URIs, so the app can show storage usage, large videos, screenshots where applicable, and downloaded videos.
  • Audio and document metadata, including filenames, sizes, timestamps, mime types, and content URIs, so the app can show storage usage and large or removable files.
  • Download, APK, junk, and empty-folder paths or metadata when needed to review and delete those items from device storage.
  • Installed app information visible through Android launcher queries, including app name, package name, icon, version, install date, and estimated size, so the app can show the app manager screen and open the Android uninstall flow when you request it.
  • Local preference data such as selected language, theme mode, scan sensitivity, large-file threshold, and cleanup history, stored on your device so the app remembers your settings.

    • Permissions the app may request

  • Photo, video, audio, or storage-related permissions so the app can analyze media and files on your device.
  • All files access (including Android's MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission where supported) may be requested only when required for core file management functionality such as junk cleanup, APK review, download review, empty-folder review, and broader storage analysis of non-media files.
  • Notification permission where required by Android so foreground scan or cleanup status can be shown to you.

    • How information is used

  • To analyze storage usage and show storage categories, duplicate files, similar photos, large files, screenshots, downloads, junk files, empty folders, APK files, and installed apps.
  • To let you review items before deletion, trashing, or uninstall requests.
  • To perform deletion, trash, uninstall, or cleanup actions only after you initiate them.
  • To remember your settings and keep cleanup history on the device.
  • To access non-media files or folders outside app-specific storage only when needed for the file-management features described in this policy.

    • On-device processing and data transfer

    Clear Space is intended to process storage and cleanup data locally on your device.

    The app does not require account creation to use its core features.

    The app does not sell your personal data.

    Based on the current shipped functionality, the app does not transmit your files, photo library, video library, document contents, or installed-app inventory to developer-operated servers for storage analysis.

    If the app requests Android's MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission, it is intended only for the on-device file-management and cleanup features described in this policy, not for advertising or unrelated data collection.

    Some optional actions you trigger may involve third-party platform services, for example:

  • Sending feedback by email through your email app.
  • Opening a store listing through Google Play.
  • Requesting an in-app review through Google Play services.
  • Sharing the app link through Android sharing targets you choose.

    • Advertising and third-party SDKs

    Clear Space may display ads served by Google AdMob.

    If ads are enabled in the version of the app you use, Google and its partners may collect or process data such as advertising identifiers, device and app information, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, ad interactions, diagnostics, and performance information in order to provide, personalize, measure, or protect advertising.

    This advertising-related data processing is performed by Google Mobile Ads SDK and related ad technology providers under their own terms and privacy practices.

    For more information, please review Google's privacy information and partner disclosures in addition to this policy.

    Data sharing and disclosure

    The app does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

    The app does not share storage-analysis data with third parties except where disclosure is required to complete an action you explicitly request or where required by law.

    If you trigger an external action such as email, app sharing, opening Google Play, or Android uninstall flows, the relevant platform or app may receive data necessary to complete that action.

    If ads are shown, advertising-related data may be collected or received by Google AdMob and its partners as described above.

    Data retention

    Settings and cleanup history are stored locally on your device until you remove app data, uninstall the app, or the app clears that information.

    Temporary scan caches may be refreshed, replaced, or removed during later scans or cleanup actions.

    Security

    The app is designed to minimize data collection and keep storage analysis on-device whenever possible.

    Access to files and media depends on Android permission controls and platform storage APIs.

    No security measure is perfect, but the app is designed to avoid unnecessary transmission of storage-analysis data off the device.

    Your choices and controls

  • You can deny or revoke permissions in Android Settings.
  • You can review items before choosing to delete, trash, or uninstall them.
  • You can clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored settings and cleanup history.
  • You can use Android and Google settings, where available, to manage ad personalization and related advertising preferences.

    • Children's privacy

    The app is not directed to children and should be used only in accordance with applicable local law.

    Changes to this policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, the revised version will include a new effective date.
