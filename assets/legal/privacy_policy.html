Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Clear Space is designed to help you review storage usage, large files, duplicate media, screenshots, downloads, and installed apps on your device.

This privacy policy describes how the app handles information based on the current in-app functionality.

Information the app accesses

  • Photos, videos, audio, documents, downloads, and other file metadata needed to scan storage and present cleanup suggestions.
  • Installed app information needed to show app names, icons, versions, install dates, and estimated sizes in the app manager.
  • Local settings such as language, theme, scan preferences, and cleanup history stored on your device.

    • How information is used

  • To analyze storage usage and show categories, duplicate files, similar photos, junk files, screenshots, downloads, and large files.
  • To perform actions that you explicitly request, such as deleting files, moving files to trash, or opening the uninstall flow for an app.
  • To remember your preferences and keep local cleanup history on the device.

    • On-device processing

    Storage scanning and file analysis are intended to run on the device. The app does not require you to create an account to use these core features.

    Sharing and disclosure

    The app does not sell your personal data.

    The app may interact with platform features or third-party services only when you explicitly trigger them, such as sending feedback by email, opening a store page, requesting an in-app review, or sharing the app link.

    Permissions

    The app may request access to photos, videos, audio, storage, notifications, or all files access when needed for storage scanning, cleanup, and background operations.

    You can deny or revoke permissions in system settings, but some features may stop working correctly if those permissions are unavailable.

    Data retention

    Preference data and cleanup history are stored locally on your device until you remove the app data, uninstall the app, or the app clears that information.

    Children's privacy

    The app is not intended for children under the age required by your local law to provide consent on their own.

    Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the revised version will be included in the app with a new effective date.
